Hietzu's Dice Game

Welcome to my dice game. Let's go over the setup. When you press play, the game will prompt you with a window, where you will select the amount of players. After that you can input the players names. Then there will be a prompt, which allows you to play to a custom point value. Now you are ready to play.

The Rules

The rules are very simple. Press the "Throw Dice" button to throw the dice. you will get the point value added to your total. If you roll doubles, you will get double your points for that roll. If you throw a 1, you get 0 points for that turn. If you roll two 1's, you get 25 points for that roll. To end your turn, press the "End Turn" button. First player to the point value you chose at the beginning, wins.
